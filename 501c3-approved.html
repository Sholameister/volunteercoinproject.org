💼 Official 501(c)(3) Public Charity

Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF)

✅ We're IRS-Approved

VCPF is officially recognized as a tax-exempt public charity under Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3). This means:

  • All donations made to VCPF are 100% tax-deductible
  • We are eligible to receive grants, bequests, and corporate gifts
  • We are legally classified as a nonprofit charitable organization

📊 What This Means for Donors

When you donate to VCPF — whether in cash, crypto, or other assets — you:

  • Can claim a tax deduction for the full donation amount
  • Help fund our mission to reward verifiable acts of kindness
  • Receive a donation receipt for your records

💻 Crypto Donations & Tax-Free Use

  • ✅ are treated like gifts — VCPF pays no taxes when selling them
  • ✅ Tokens earned through volunteering are distributed as part of our mission (not taxable income)
  • ❌ We do not engage in speculative trading or operate a crypto exchange
  • ✅ All token use (LVBTN, SYNCM) is tied directly to public good and nonprofit transparency
