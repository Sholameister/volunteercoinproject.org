Proof Of Impact!

LVBTN & SYNCM – Two Tokens. One Mission. Infinite Impact.

SYNCM rewards verified service. LVBTN amplifies your rewards and opens doors to unique opportunities. Together, they power a blockchain ecosystem where good deeds translate into tangible value.

💠 SYNCM — Earned by Action

SYNCM is earned for each hour of verified volunteer service. It's your daily “Proof-of-Impact.”

  • 🧠 Smart: Logged with time, photo, and geolocation
  • 🔐 Secure: Only issued to KYC-verified volunteers
  • 💵 Flexible: Redeemable, stakeable, or tradable (with restrictions)

💖 LVBTN — Awarded Monthly with Heart

LVBTN is a scarce, premium utility token. It's awarded on the 1st of each month to verified volunteers:

  • 🥉 Tier 1: 1 LVBTN
  • 🥈 Tier 2: 2 LVBTN
  • 🥇 Tier 3: 3 LVBTN
  • Tier 4: 3 LVBTN
  • Tier 5: 3 LVBTN

🧩 Use Cases for LVBTN

  • 🚕 Rides for volunteers (Uber, Lyft, etc.)
  • 🎁 Donate or gift to others in need
  • 💵 Exchange (upcoming partnerships where available)
  • 🎮 Access to special games, raffles, events
  • 🌾 Multipliers for Staking, farming, and earning rewards
  • 🪙 Entry into access-only programs

🔄 The Ecosystem Flow

  1. Serve and earn SYNCM
  2. KYC Check is a must to receive monthly LVBTN
  3. Use LVBTN to enhance your rewards or exchange for value
  4. Stake to climb tiers and boost earnings

Stability: Both tokens are backed! LVBTN by BTC and SYNCM by SOL and are controlled by a transparent, mission-driven foundation.
