✨ Public Statement of Legality & Transparency

Issued by the Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF)

At the Volunteer Coin Project Foundation, transparency is our virtue — and integrity is our foundation. We are proud to operate as a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit public charity, committed to uplifting communities through service, generosity, and the power of participation.

We believe in a future where time and impact are recognized in meaningful, real-world ways. That’s why we created the LoveButton Token (LVBTN) and SYNCM, utility tokens that are earned — not bought, through volunteer service, compassion, and verified acts of good.

💗 Our Mission Is Clear:

  • To reward love in action — not speculation
  • To document impact transparently — not privately
  • To empower volunteers and communities with tools that give back

💎 Our Commitments:

  • LVBTN and SYNCM are earned through time and service
  • Tokens hold no speculative promise — they are used as a symbol of verified impact
  • Over 89 million LVBTN tokens have been permanently removed from supply
  • All rewards are distributed based on timestamped, photo-verified sessions
  • Token supply is fixed and transparent, with daily/weekly logs and full accountability
  • Our treasury is backed by Bitcoin, Solana, and gold for stability and redemption
  • We operate on principles of faith, fairness, and stewardship

🌍 We Serve With Love

The LoveButton movement is more than just a token — it’s a commitment to building a world where love is rewarded, time is honored, and people are empowered to help others freely, without financial barriers.

Our tools are built to uplift, not enrich. Our ecosystem is designed for giving, healing, rebuilding, and thriving.

We welcome support from individuals, families, churches, nonprofits, businesses, and communities who share our heart for service.

“Your Time, Your Impact, Your Reward.”

We invite all who believe in a brighter future to join us — not in pursuit of profit, but in pursuit of purpose.
