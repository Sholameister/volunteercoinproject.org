LVBTN is one of the safest cryptocurrencies in circulation today, designed with a mission-driven, closed-loop utility structure.

Unlike speculative tokens, LVBTN operates in a secure, permissioned ecosystem where every token earned is tied to real-world action and verified impact.

Volunteers earn LVBTN through verified acts of service, logged via photo proof, timestamping, and wallet/KYC verification — all fully integrated with Firebase and blockchain technologies.

There are no public liquidity pools or external trading APIs, ensuring value stability and protection from market bots or manipulation.

All redemptions occur through direct interaction with the Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF), making LVBTN a truly community-backed currency rooted in purpose and transparency.

This is Proof of Impact in action — where crypto meets compassion, and service fuels sustainability.
