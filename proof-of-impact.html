🔹 What is Proof of Impact?

Proof of Impact (PoI) is a next-generation consensus and reward model where tokens are earned through verifiable acts of good — not by mining, staking, or speculation.

Instead of using machines to prove worth, PoI uses real-world service

🔹 How It Works with VCPF

  1. User completes KYC and connects a wallet.
  2. They upload a “before” photo, click Start Volunteering, and begin their service task.
  3. They upload an “after” photo and click Stop Volunteering.
  4. The system logs timestamp, geolocation, tier, and wallet.
  5. Tokens are calculated and automatically distributed at 23:59 each day.
