Volunteer Tiers – BlueGrid LiveMesh

"Proof-of-Impact"

"Your Time, Your Impact, Your Reward"

The Volunteer Coin Project Foundation rewards impact through a tiered system. Each tier unlocks new opportunities, responsibilities, and rewards. Volunteers start simple, then choose how far they want to go. Transparency is our virtue.

Tier 1 – Explorer

  • Email signup only
  • Access to SYNCM games + marketplace preview
  • Rewards: 5 SYNCM hourly only (no LVBTN)

Tier 2 – Participant

  • Bluetooth ON + volunteer check-in
  • Access to marketplace + events
  • Rewards: 10 SYNCM hourly only (no LVBTN)

Tier 3 – Volunteer

  • KYC verified
  • Full volunteer dashboard + token tracking
  • Rewards: 1 LVBTN monthly + 15 SYNCM/hour

Tier 4 – Responder

  • KYC + CPR/BLS certified
  • Emergency volunteer roles + responder badge
  • Rewards: 2 LVBTN monthly + 20 SYNCM/hour

Tier 5 – Mentor (Ultimate Goal)

  • KYC + 50+ service hours logged
  • Governance voting rights + advanced recognition
  • Rewards: 3 LVBTN monthly + 25 SYNCM/hour

BlueShip Tier – Teachers & Students

  • Teachers: certified educators (no KYC required)
  • Students: parental signature + release form
  • Safe participation in school/community programs
  • Rewards: 25 SYNCM hourly only ( LVBTN)
