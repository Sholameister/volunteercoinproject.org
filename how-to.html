How the Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF) Works

"Proof of Impact" — Earn by Serving, Grow by Giving

The Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF) is a nonprofit initiative that rewards service and generosity through blockchain innovation. It empowers individuals, families, and communities to uplift each other through verified acts of service.

Key Concepts

  • LVBTN: Premier token earned monthly by verified volunteers (2/3/4 tokens per month depending on tier).
  • SYNCM: Earned hourly (5/10/15 tokens per hour) for each verified volunteer shift.
  • Proof of Impact: Each act of service is logged with geolocation, timestamp, and photos as verified proof.

How to Participate

  1. Sign up on the platform and complete KYC (student ID may be accepted for minors).
  2. Begin volunteer shifts, logging time and uploading before/after service photos.
  3. Earn SYNCM hourly and receive monthly LVBTN token drops.
  4. Use LVBTN to redeem rewards, stake for bonuses, join raffles, or donate to causes.

Volunteer Incentives

  • Monthly LVBTN Drops:
    • Tier 1: 2 LVBTN
    • Tier 2: 3 LVBTN
    • Tier 3: 4 LVBTN
  • Hourly SYNCM Rewards:
    • Tier 1: 5 SYNCM/hour
    • Tier 2: 10 SYNCM/hour
    • Tier 3: 15 SYNCM/hour
  • Referral Bonuses: Earn tokens by referring new verified volunteers.
  • Student Volunteers: Can log community service hours and donate their tokens to schools or causes of their choice.

Our Mission

VCPF is building a new economy rooted in compassion and accountability. Every token is backed by action — every action makes a real-world difference.

Transparency is our virtue. Your Time, Your Impact, Your Reward.
