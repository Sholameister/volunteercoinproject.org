$1M Vision: National Rollout Plan

Prepared: August 4, 2025

  • Launch a national volunteer tracking and rewards system powered by LVBTN tokens
  • Onboard 5,000+ verified, KYC-compliant volunteers within the first 12 months
  • Expand staking, redemption, and BTC/gold-backed treasury systems
  • Deploy token-fueled incentives for service, outreach, and sustainability
