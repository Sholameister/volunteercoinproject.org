1. Executive Summary

Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (VCPF) operates a closed, structured rewards model—Proof-of-Impact—that verifies volunteer activity and delivers transparent on-chain rewards. Two tokens power the system:

  • LVBTN — a scarce, premium token earned monthly; used for staking, multipliers, raffles, and premium redemptions.
  • SYNCM — the global utility token earned hourly for service; fuels redemptions, farming, and NGO programs.

3. Tokens & Utilities

LVBTN — LoveButton Token

  • Nature: scarce/premium; earned through impact, not freely sold.
  • Utility: stake to earn SYNCM, access multipliers, raffles, premium redemptions, and special events.
  • Cadence: monthly drops by tier (1/2/3 LVBTN).

SYNCM — SyncMesh Token

  • Nature: abundant utility; rewards volunteers hourly and powers NGO programs.
  • Utility: redemptions, farming, multipliers, donations to causes.
  • Cadence: earned hourly at tiered rates.

5. Proof-of-Impact Workflow

  1. KYC & Wallet → user verifies via Blockpass and connects a wallet.
  2. Start Session → before-photo, timestamp, geolocation.
  3. Stop Session → after-photo, timestamp, LVBTN/SYNCM calculation.
  4. Verification → admin or automated checks approve logs.
  5. Distribution → SYNCM hourly; LVBTN monthly (1st of month by tier).
All logs stored in Firestore; photos in Firebase Storage; summaries visible in the volunteer dashboard.

7. Reserves, Ledgers & Transparency

  • 30% of all incoming funds are allocated to reserves (BTC, SOL, and Gold) to support long-term stability.
  • Public pages list: burned tokens, reserve balances, and monthly distributions.
  • Donor and volunteer summaries published on a recurring schedule.
Backed by hard assets + transparent reporting to reinforce trust in a closed, structured reward system.

8. MeshSync Beacons (Resilience Layer)

MeshSync Corp builds a Bluetooth-mesh, offline-capable network to keep communications and Proof-of-Impact synchronization alive during outages.

  • EMP-aware hardware, solar-friendly power, neighborhood-first deployment.
  • Beacon registry (IDs, locations, hosts, incentives) with proof photos.
  • Planned features: offline message relays, location map, heartbeat health.
From backyard prototype → neighborhood → city: the first disaster-resilient foundation powered by its own mesh.

10. Governance

  • VCPF Board oversight for treasury, reserves, distributions, and KYC policy.
  • Operational transparency: monthly reports; audited ledgers as feasible.
  • Conflict-of-interest and safeguarding policies for volunteer programs.

11. Risks & Disclaimers

Tokens are issued as rewards for verifiable service in a closed, structured system. They are not marketed as securities or investment products. No promises of profit. Market, regulatory, technical, and operational risks apply.
  • Regulatory: compliance requirements may evolve; KYC/AML enforced.
  • Operational: downtime, fraud attempts, or beacon failures may impact rewards.
  • Market: external valuations (if any) can be volatile and outside VCPF control.
  • Custody: users are responsible for wallet security and backups.
