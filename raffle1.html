How the Raffle Works

  1. Purchase a numbered ticket through Zeffy. A donation is suggested; entries are voluntary.
  2. All ticket numbers are entered into a random drawing conducted by VCPF.
  3. We will livestream the draw on PLATFORM + LINK at DATE & TIME (TIME ZONE).
  4. Winners will be posted at vcpf.org/raffle-results and notified by email.

Eligibility & Notes

  • Open to residents where charitable raffles are permitted. Must be 18+.
  • Employees, officers, and directors of VCPF may be restricted from entering at VCPF’s discretion.
  • Prizes are delivered as Solana-based tokens (SPL). Winners will need a compatible wallet (e.g., Phantom/Solflare). We can assist with setup.

Drawing Transparency

Zeffy processes entries but does not conduct the drawing. VCPF will run the drawing publicly using a verifiable random method and will publish: (1) a timestamped ticket list, (2) the recorded drawing link, and (3) the winners list.
