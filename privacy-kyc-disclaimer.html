Privacy Policy & KYC Disclaimer

Effective Date: May 2025

The Volunteer Coin Project Foundation (“VCPF”) collects limited personal information as part of our Know Your Customer (KYC) process to comply with ethical token distribution practices and to prevent fraud.

All information submitted through our forms, including identification documents and wallet addresses, will be used solely for verification purposes and will never be sold or shared with third parties without your consent, unless legally required by law enforcement.

We take your privacy seriously and will store your data using encrypted cloud storage and internal-only access controls.

By submitting your information, you consent to our collection and use of your personal data for KYC compliance during the LVBTN token presale.

If you have any concerns or would like your information removed after the presale ends, please contact info@volunteercoinproject.org.

Thank you for your trust and support as we build a transparent, community-driven future together.
