LoveButton Token (LVBTN) – FAQ

What do I get for holding LVBTN tokens?

While LVBTN is not a security and does not promise financial returns, we do believe in recognizing those who contribute to the mission. That includes volunteers, builders, and early holders who help grow the ecosystem.

Holders may be eligible for future community-based perks, such as:

  • Access to exclusive features
  • Priority on new projects or airdrops
  • Governance voting or advisory roles
  • Bonus opportunities tied to real-world impact

All benefits are utility-based, optional, and not tied to passive income or profit guarantees. LVBTN exists to reward purpose, not speculation.

Legal Disclaimer on Token Use and Holder Benefits

The LoveButton Token (LVBTN) is a utility token designed to incentivize real-world volunteerism and community service. It is not an investment contract and does not constitute a security.

Any future recognition of token holders will be conducted as part of a non-financial engagement strategy — including access to ecosystem tools, limited governance participation, digital badges, or early beta invites.

There are no guarantees, dividends, or expectations of profit. The token's purpose is to support transparency, community trust, and mission-driven rewards tied to active participation, not passive holding.
